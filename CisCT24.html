MORE ABOUT VALENCIA

Valencia, the land of flowers and the Fallas, is one of the largest and most important cities in Spain, and one of the most alive. Being located on the shore of the Mediterranean Sea, y ou will find the beaches in the very heart of the city. It is not surprising that, especially during the summer, a large number of tourists visit it. Well, if you want a beach holiday with fantastic possibilities to practice any sport and enjoy a brilliant nightlife, along with a wide cultural offer, then Valencia is the place for it.

  • Important international fairs confirm that Valencia is one of the most dynamic cities in Spain. Walking through its streets and enjoying the abundant possibilities that the capital offers is something for which the hours can pass without realizing it. If after that, you feel hungry, then it is time to try the most famous dish in Spain exactly where it was born, "Paella Valenciana".
  • Furthermore, Valencia is of course the city where El Cid, the national hero, fought against the moors during the "Reconquista". Many popular festivals in the city and surrounding towns still remember events from this time. But in this chapter we cannot forget, above all, "Las Fallas", impressive festivals of light and fire that will impress anyone who passes through here coinciding with these dates.
  • In recent years Valencia has acquired additional prominence at an international level. Important cultural events have contributed to this; such as the construction of the City of Arts and Sciences and important international sports competitions or the Formula I Grand Prix that is held on a spectacular urban circuit that runs through the Port area.
